The benchmarks and their description are now part of the ASOL distribution. The project is hosted on sourcforge.net: ASOL -- Affine Solver.

Although ASOL is still just a proof-of-concept implementation, it finds even those solutions that are missed by commercial solvers. The algorithm is presented in my most recent manuscript entitled Computing multiple steady states in homogeneous azeotropic and ideal two-product distillation (DOI). Further implementation details are given in Computation of an extractive distillation column with affine arithmetic (DOI).

The parts presenting the algorithms are human readable and are independent of chemical engineering.
